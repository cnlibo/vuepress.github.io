    # Introduction

    Hydraulic valves can be used to control the flow of fluid in a circuit as well as the quantity of fluid power. As a result, the pressure and flow rate in various portions of the circuit would be controlled by these valves. Hydraulic valves can regulate system pressure, relieves the pressure on the pump or lowers the temperature of the oil before it enters the hydraulic circuit.
